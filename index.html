About Us

The CMU Blockchain Group is a software develoment focused group of students dedicated to bringing high-quality presentations and workshops to the students of CMU and citizens of Pittsburgh overall. We want to equip people with the foundational knowledge to reason blockchain developments from an engineering perspective, taking into account technical, economic and policy factors. We want to build a community where students and faculty can come together to network and learn from each other, as well as to collaborate on new and existing ideas.

Education

We host whitepaper reading sessions and participate in Cylab's Blockchain Speaker Series.

Experience

We sharpen our technical skills by working on projects and participating in blockchain hackathons and competitions.
